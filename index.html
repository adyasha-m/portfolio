About

I started out with training models, writing scripts, and working on AI research. Along the way, I realized the part I enjoyed most wasn't the model itself, but understanding the data behind it: cleaning messy datasets, finding patterns, asking better questions, and turning results into decisions. That curiosity eventually led me to data and product analytics, where I get to combine technical problem-solving with product thinking.

Questions I Keep Coming Back To

-- 03 · projects

Projects

RapidKart — Quick Commerce Product Analytics

Problem

Understand where users drop off in the ordering journey and identify operational factors affecting conversion and long-term retention.

Approach

Built an end-to-end analytics pipeline on 1M+ simulated events using SQL, Python and PySpark. Performed funnel, cohort and retention analysis before visualizing insights in Power BI.

Outcome

Identified a 42.8% checkout drop-off, discovered a -0.615 correlation between delivery time and conversion, and segmented 10,000 users into behavioral cohorts.

Tools

SQL • Python • PySpark • Power BI

Pizza Sales Dashboard

Problem

Analyze sales performance to uncover revenue drivers, customer demand patterns and opportunities for better inventory planning.

Approach

Queried transactional data with SQL, performed Pareto analysis and built an interactive Power BI dashboard to explore sales trends and business KPIs.

Outcome

Found that nearly 80% of revenue came from a small subset of products and that weekday sales were 168% higher than weekends, with peak demand during lunch and evening hours.

Tools

SQL • Power BI • Excel
